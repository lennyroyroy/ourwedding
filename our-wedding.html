Our Story

While we already had our marriage ceremony and are married, our wedding reception was delayed for a long time because of the COVID-19 pandemic. We wanted to be safe and were willing to wait for the right moment to throw a large party for our families and friends. That time has now come and we are so excited to finally celebrate with our loved ones. Our reception will reflect a mix of Afghan and Colombian culture, so be prepared to eat and dance to Afghani/Colombian music all night long!

Please note this will be an alcohol-free event.
